Welcome to ACST

Empowering Youth for Sustainable Tourism and Conservation in Rwanda

Alliance for Conservation and Sustainable Tourism

At ACST, we are committed to fostering collaboration among stakeholders in the conservation and tourism sectors in Rwanda. Explore our current projects:

ACST

We are dedicated to promoting conservation and sustainable tourism in Rwanda. Our mission is to protect natural resources, support local communities, and foster sustainable development through education and engagement.

Our Projects

We have various ongoing projects aimed at conservation, community empowerment, and sustainable tourism. From wildlife protection to community education programs, our projects make a real difference.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a global network of like-minded organizations, businesses, and individuals dedicated to advancing conservation efforts while enhancing the tourism experience sustainably. We aim to ensure that tourism practices respect and protect the environment, contribute to the well-being of local communities, and preserve the cultural heritage of Rwanda.

Our Focus

We place a special emphasis on empowering the young generation, including women, youth, and people living with disabilities, to ensure that we create long-lasting positive impacts on our planet. By engaging these key groups, we aspire to foster a more inclusive and sustainable future.

Join Us

We invite you to join us in our mission to promote conservation and sustainable tourism in Rwanda. Whether you are an organization, business, or individual, there are many ways to get involved and make a difference. Together, we can create a more sustainable and inclusive future for all.

Our

EDUCATION

We create and organize projects and activities, aimed to educate communities, students, and actors in conservation and tourism sectors. We partner with educational institutions and other organizations to discuss and address conservation and tourism related issues.

ACTIVE CITIZENSHIP

We build trust and understanding at the community level by supporting people to take action on issues they care about in accordance with conservation and sustainable tourism principles.

MOTHER EARTH

We support our planet by organizing eco-campaigns and informing sessions about global warming, pollution, deforestation, and recycling. We also focus on ecosystem planning and restoration, restoring degraded habitats to enhance biodiversity and ecosystem services.

HUMAN RIGHTS

We promote and organize activities to combat social exclusion and human rights abuses at the community level. This includes advocacy and awareness campaigns, raising public awareness on conservation and sustainable tourism through various media channels.
