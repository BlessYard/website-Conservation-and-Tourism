Opportunities

Training/Courses

ACST offers a variety of training programs and courses aimed at educating and empowering youth in conservation and sustainable tourism. Our courses cover topics such as eco-friendly tourism practices, environmental conservation, and community engagement. In addition to our own offerings, we compile opportunities from local and international sources, providing participants with a comprehensive selection of expert-led workshops and hands-on training experiences.

Funding

Discover funding opportunities with ACST! We provide financial support for projects and initiatives that align with our mission of promoting sustainable tourism and conservation. Our funding programs include ACST grants as well as a curated list of local and international funding sources. Whether you are an entrepreneur with a green business idea or a community leader with a conservation project, we help you find the financial resources needed to bring your vision to life.
