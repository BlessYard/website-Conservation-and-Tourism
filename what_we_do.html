What We Do

Our Programs

  • Education on Conservation and Sustainable Tourism: We offer educational programs that raise awareness about the importance of conservation and sustainable tourism practices. These initiatives aim to equip young people with the knowledge and skills needed to become advocates for the environment.
  • Advocacy and Awareness Campaigns: Our campaigns focus on promoting responsible tourism and conservation efforts. Through advocacy, we seek to influence policies and practices that support sustainable development in the tourism sector.
  • Community Engagement and Empowerment: We work closely with local communities to develop and implement conservation and sustainable tourism projects. By empowering community members, we help them to actively participate in and benefit from these initiatives.
  • Ecosystem Planning and Restoration: We undertake projects that aim to restore and maintain the health of local ecosystems. These efforts are crucial for preserving biodiversity and ensuring the sustainability of tourism activities.
  • Empowerment of Rwandan Entrepreneurs: We support local entrepreneurs by helping them to develop and scale up green business models. These businesses not only contribute to climate action but also provide economic opportunities for local communities.

We Encourage Conservation and Tourism Practices

Fostering collaboration among stakeholders in the conservation and tourism sectors. Through innovative partnerships, advocacy, and education, it aims to promote responsible tourism practices that respect and protect the environment, support local cultures, and contribute to the well-being of communities. Its mission is to create a global network of like-minded organizations, businesses, and individuals dedicated to advancing conservation efforts while enhancing the tourism experience sustainably. Young generations are the key focus to make sure we build long lasting impacts on the earth.
