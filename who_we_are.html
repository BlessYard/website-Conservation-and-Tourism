Who we are

About Us

Welcome to the Alliance for Conservation and Sustainable Tourism (ACST)!

At ACST, we are committed to fostering collaboration among stakeholders in the conservation and tourism sectors in Rwanda. As a youth-based non-profit organization, we strive to create innovative partnerships, advocate for responsible tourism practices, and provide educational resources that promote the protection of our natural environment and support local cultures.

Our Mission

At ASCT, our mission is to global network of like-minded organizations, businesses, and individuals dedicated to advancing conservation efforts while enhancing the tourism experience sustainably.

We aim to ensure that tourism practices respect and protect the environment, contribute to the well-being of local communities, and preserve the cultural heritage of Rwanda.

Our Focus

We place a special emphasis on empowering the young generation, including women, youth, and people living with disabilities, to ensure that we create long-lasting positive impacts on our planet.

By engaging these key groups, we aspire to foster a more inclusive and sustainable future.
